Scheduling

Private lessons are taught on a weekly basis for 30 or 45 minutes depending on the
student’s age and goals.  More advanced students may request a 60-minute session.  
Pittsburgh Saxophone Lessons also offers the option of supplementary chamber music
(group performances) for those interested, and encourages participation in public
performances and competitions.

Curriculum

While each lesson is tailored to the individual needs of each student, all students are
encouraged to:

-Learn to read or improve their ability to read music notation.

-Develop performance skills, self-confidence, and express their creativity.

-Build their repertoire from music of all styles and periods.

-Learn to play for personal pleasure or for public performance.
